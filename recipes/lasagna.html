Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a picture of lasagna. I believe it is an italian dish, but the truth is you can never be too sure these days. Good luck and have fun.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Basil leaves
  • Meat
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Place layer of pasta
  2. Add cheese and meat
  3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 until desired thickness
  4. Add cheese on top
  5. Add basil leaves on top
  6. Cook at 400F for 15 minutes
