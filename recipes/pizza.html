Mario's Pizza Recipe

Description

This is a picture of an italian pizza that has cheese. Cheese is good for you. Therefore, you should eat pizza everyday.

Ingredients

  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Basil Leaves
  • Kosher Salt

Steps

  1. Roll out your dough
  2. Put Pizza Sauce on flattened Dough
  3. Place Mozzarella, Basil, and Salt on Pizza
  4. Heat at 400F for 10 minutes
