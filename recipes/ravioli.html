Ravioli Recipe

Description

This is a ravioli. Sahil loves ravioli. He always orders it when he goes to olive garden

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Ricotta
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Bring pot of water to boil
  2. Add Ravioli and cook for 12 minutes
  3. Heat Pan with butter
  4. Add garlic, cheese, olive oil and pasta to pan
  5. Cook for a few minutes on low heat
